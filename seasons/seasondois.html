In the second season of Arcane, the fragile balance between Piltover and Zaun begins to unravel as the stakes grow higher. The aftermath of the events from the first season leaves both cities on the brink of chaos, with political intrigue and personal vendettas pushing characters to their limits. Vi and Jinx continue their turbulent journey, each grappling with their own identities and the deepening divide between them. As new alliances are forged and old enemies resurface, the battle for control over the future of both cities intensifies.
