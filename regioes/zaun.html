Zaun

Zaun, the shadowy and industrial Undercity of Arcane, is a place of stark contrasts and raw energy. Nestled beneath the prosperous city of Piltover, Zaun is a world shaped by hardship, ingenuity, and rebellion. Its skyline is dominated by sprawling factories, towering chimneys, and glowing neon signs that cut through the ever-present haze of smog. The city hums with the sounds of machinery and life, a testament to its residents' relentless drive to survive and thrive despite their challenges. Life in Zaun is not easy. Poverty and crime are rampant, and the city is ruled as much by powerful criminal organizations as by its own chaotic rhythms. Yet, amid the grime and danger, Zaun is a cradle of invention and unbridled creativity. Its scientists and alchemists work without the restrictions of Piltover's strict regulations, leading to groundbreaking—and often perilous—discoveries in technology and hextech.
