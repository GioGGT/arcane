Piltover

Piltover, the illustrious "City of Progress" in Arcane, stands as a beacon of innovation, elegance, and opportunity. Its gleaming spires, polished cobblestone streets, and bustling marketplaces reflect a city that values order, advancement, and wealth. Piltover is the heart of trade and invention, with its prestigious Academy attracting the brightest minds from across the world.
